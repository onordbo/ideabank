Guerilla Marketing

People take the internet for granted so that's why we decided to bring that reality to them. The idea is to prompt people with an error message when they open up their browsers at popular Wi-Fi hotspots. It is merely a temporary prank to get users to briefly think about what it is like to live without internet - the Wi-Fi still works and will eventually load.
