Inspiring Mentorship

It’s the world's’ deadliest war and the ones fighting are children. The right to a childhood with possibilities of creating your own future and living up to your potential is something that every child should have the right to do.

Previous child soldiers and victims of such will be given the opportunity to participate in a mentorship program with the goal of smoothly transitioning back into everyday life over a period of time. The program will consist of several different steps where you will follow an older mentor that has previously gone through the same experiences as you or similar to yours. Together there will be opportunities to create plans of finding your way back to living a life where it’s up to you to find and create your dreams, goals and your unique way in life.
