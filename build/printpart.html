Print Your Part

Chronic mismanagement and internal conflicts has affected the development of infrastructure and transportation possibilities in Congo. The so-called “National route” in Congo makes the transportation process take several months. If your car breaks down, or hospital equipment is dysfunctional because of a spear part missing, this can have fatal consequences.

By investing in 3D printing hubs by using online communities like Kickstarter or companies developing 3D printers for printers, entrepreneurs can produce their own spare parts. People in the business can throw workshops to provide Congolese entrepreneurs with the knowledge and software they need.
