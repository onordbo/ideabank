Hear Their Voice

People have heard of “Be their Voice” but in order to have a conversation for change the message has to be heard.

The whistle represents their voice which naturally lead us to creating a symbolic icon that represents the fact their message has been heard. To start this new revenue stream for Falling Whistles, we suggest sending out an earring to spokespeople who have been helpful in the past. People who have made Congo’s voice heard, like Rihanna have done.
