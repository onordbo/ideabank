Send Your Phone Home

While western countries have experienced the evolution of day-to-day technology, African countries have to some extent skipped the PC evolution. Cell Phones are now a vital part of their economical and social development.

To raise awareness around the fact that the materials in our devices come from countries like Congo, we want people to “send their phone home”. Swedish graduates from high school get a choice of donating their school computers and old phones to educational and entrepreneurial causes in Congo.
