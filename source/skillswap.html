Skillswap

People in Congo have an urge to learn as much as possible, whether it be starting their own business or kids that want to learn so they can have better opportunities. A problem they encounter is the limited technological resources that exist and the lack of knowledge to develop and sustain a brighter future for themselves. Our solution is to create local workshops where you have the ability to learn how to both learn for yourself and how to teach others what you know. These workshops can be created on their own to spread and share knowledge.

In order to achieve equality for all we must also ensure that all have equal access to education whether it be through technology or in class learning. To increase our chances as much as possible we believe in creating an educational learning platform where kids in the vital learning years can access simplified learnings tools and “how to” videos. To solve this the opportunity of sharing your knowledge from Sweden directly to the entrepreneurs in Congolese communities will also exist. You can easily pass on your knowledge by sharing videos of how to do things on the Skill Swap-platform. Money only goes so far, spreading your skills and knowledge is a major resource in developing a learning model that will facilitate business progress.
